Follow the Money

"Who funds corporate media?" with your favorite hosts.

About

The Story

Five billionaires and two family dynasties control the majority of American news. Most people consume media every day without knowing who owns it, what else those owners invest in, or how that shapes what gets reported.

Follow the Money is a series featuring two (agent) hosts who investigate who owns corporate media, discussing their findings on the money trail, ownership data, and biases of these publications, using real-time data to highlight current events and historical data for a full investigation.

How It Works

Claude powers both personalities and searches the web for real-time ownership data. Cartesia gives each character a distinct voice. Notion stores the curated ownership database, queried alongside live web search.

6 publications. 3 data sources per conversation. Every investigation is different.
